# Okta, Inc.

Okta is an American identity and access management company headquartered in San Francisco. The company provides cloud-based identity solutions for enterprises.

## History

Founded in 2009 by Todd McKinnon and Frederic Kerrest, former Salesforce executives who saw the need for cloud-native identity management.

## Leadership

- Todd McKinnon, Co-founder and CEO
- Frederic Kerrest, Co-founder and COO

## Products

### Workforce Identity
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Universal Directory
- Lifecycle Management
- API Access Management

### Customer Identity
- Auth0 (acquired 2021 for $6.5 billion)
- Customer authentication
- Developer-focused identity APIs

### Okta Identity Governance
Identity governance and administration capabilities.

## Technology

### Integration Network
Over 7,000 pre-built integrations with applications.

### Zero Trust
Okta's platform supports zero trust security architectures with continuous authentication.

## Auth0 Acquisition

The 2021 acquisition of Auth0 expanded Okta's customer identity capabilities and developer-focused offerings.

## Market Position

Okta is the leader in independent identity management, competing with Microsoft Azure AD and Ping Identity.

## Security Incident

In 2022, Okta disclosed a security incident involving the Lapsus$ hacking group, highlighting the critical nature of identity infrastructure security.
