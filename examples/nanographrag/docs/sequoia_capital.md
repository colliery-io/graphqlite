# Sequoia Capital

Sequoia Capital is an American venture capital firm headquartered in Menlo Park, California. Founded in 1972 by Don Valentine, it is one of the most successful and influential venture capital firms in history.

## History

Don Valentine founded Sequoia Capital in 1972 after working at Fairchild Semiconductor and National Semiconductor. The firm has been instrumental in funding some of the most successful technology companies.

## Leadership

- Roelof Botha, Managing Partner (US/Europe)
- Neil Shen, Founding Partner (China, now HongShan)
- Doug Leone, former Managing Partner

## Notable Investments

### Early Stage Successes
- Apple (1978)
- Cisco (1987)
- Oracle (1980s)
- Yahoo (1995)
- Google (1999)

### Modern Portfolio
- Airbnb
- Stripe
- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- DoorDash
- Zoom
- Klarna

## Structure Changes

In 2022, Sequoia announced a major restructuring, separating its US/Europe, China (now HongShan), and India/Southeast Asia operations into independent partnerships.

## Investment Philosophy

Sequoia is known for backing companies from seed stage through IPO, with a focus on long-term partnerships with founders.

## Assets Under Management

Sequoia manages over $85 billion across its global operations.
