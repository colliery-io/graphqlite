# Zoom Video Communications

Zoom Video Communications, Inc. is an American communications technology company headquartered in San Jose, California. The company is best known for its video conferencing platform, which became ubiquitous during the COVID-19 pandemic.

## History

Zoom was founded in 2011 by Eric Yuan, a former Cisco Webex engineer. The company launched its software in 2013 and went public in April 2019. Usage exploded during the COVID-19 pandemic as remote work became widespread.

## Leadership

- Eric Yuan, Chief Executive Officer and founder
- Kelly Steckelberg, Chief Financial Officer
- Velchamy Sankarlingam, President of Product and Engineering

## Products

### Zoom Meetings
The core video conferencing product that supports meetings of various sizes, from one-on-one calls to webinars with thousands of participants.

### Zoom Phone
Cloud-based phone system for business communications.

### Zoom Rooms
Hardware and software for conference room video conferencing.

### Zoom Team Chat
Instant messaging and collaboration features integrated with meetings.

### Zoom Contact Center
Cloud contact center solution for customer service.

### Zoom AI Companion
AI-powered features including meeting summaries and smart recordings.

## Competition

Zoom competes with:
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Meet
- Cisco Webex
- Slack (for team chat)
- RingCentral (for phone)

## Pandemic Growth

Zoom's daily meeting participants grew from 10 million in December 2019 to over 300 million in April 2020, making "Zoom" synonymous with video calling.
