# Dell Technologies Inc.

Dell Technologies is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Round Rock, Texas. One of the largest technology infrastructure companies in the world.

## History

Founded by Michael Dell in 1984 while a student at the University of Texas at Austin. The company pioneered the direct-to-consumer PC sales model.

## Leadership

- Michael Dell, Chairman and CEO
- Jeff Clarke, Vice Chairman and COO

## Business Segments

### Infrastructure Solutions Group (ISG)
- Servers (PowerEdge)
- Storage (PowerStore, PowerScale)
- Networking

### Client Solutions Group (CSG)
- Laptops (Latitude, XPS, Alienware)
- Desktops (OptiPlex)
- Workstations (Precision)

## Corporate History

### Going Private
Dell went private in 2013 with Silver Lake Partners in a $24.9 billion deal.

### EMC Acquisition
In 2016, Dell acquired EMC Corporation for $67 billion, the largest technology acquisition at the time.

### Return to Public Markets
Dell returned to public trading in 2018 via a complex transaction with VMware tracking stock.

## VMware

Dell owned approximately 81% of VMware until its spin-off in 2021.

## Direct Model

Michael Dell's direct sales model eliminated middlemen and allowed for customization, disrupting the traditional PC industry.

## AI Infrastructure

Dell has positioned itself as a key provider of AI infrastructure, partnering with NVIDIA on AI server solutions.
