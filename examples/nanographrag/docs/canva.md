# Canva Pty Ltd

Canva is an Australian graphic design platform headquartered in Sydney. The company provides online design tools accessible to non-designers.

## History

Founded in 2012 by Melanie Perkins, Cliff Obrecht, and Cameron Adams. Perkins and Obrecht previously created Fusion Books, a school yearbook design platform.

## Leadership

- Melanie Perkins, Co-founder and CEO
- Cliff Obrecht, Co-founder and COO
- Cameron Adams, Co-founder and Chief Product Officer

## Products

### Design Tools
- Drag-and-drop design editor
- Templates for various formats
- Brand kit management
- Photo and video editing
- Magic Studio (AI features)

### Enterprise
- Canva for Enterprise
- Brand management
- Collaboration tools

### Print
- Canva Print for physical products

## Business Model

Freemium model with:
- Canva Free
- Canva Pro (individual)
- Canva for Teams
- Canva for Enterprise

## Acquisitions

Notable acquisitions include:
- Pexels (stock photos)
- Pixabay (stock media)
- Kaleido.ai (background removal)
- Affinity (2024, professional design software)

## Market Position

Canva has grown to over 170 million monthly users, democratizing design for small businesses, educators, and individuals.

## Valuation

Canva reached a $40 billion valuation, making it one of Australia's most valuable private companies.
