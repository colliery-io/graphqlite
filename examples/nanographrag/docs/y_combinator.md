# Y Combinator

Y Combinator (YC) is an American startup accelerator headquartered in Mountain View, California. It is the most successful startup accelerator in the world.

## History

Founded in March 2005 by Paul Graham, Jessica Livingston, Robert Morris, and Trevor Blackwell. The first batch included Reddit and Loopt.

## Leadership

- Garry Tan, President and CEO (since 2023)
- Dalton Caldwell, Managing Director
- Michael Seibel, Managing Director
- Paul Graham, co-founder (now advisor)
- Jessica Livingston, co-founder

## Program Structure

### Batch Program
- Twice-yearly batches (Winter and Summer)
- 3-month intensive program
- Standard deal: $500,000 for 7% equity (as of 2022)
- Demo Day presentations to investors

### YC Continuity
Growth-stage fund for YC alumni companies.

## Impact

YC has funded over 4,000 companies with combined valuation exceeding $600 billion. The alumni network is one of its most valuable assets.

## Startup School

Free online program teaching startup fundamentals, extending YC's reach beyond the core accelerator program.
