# Pinterest, Inc.

Pinterest, Inc. is an American social media company headquartered in San Francisco, California. The company operates Pinterest, a visual discovery and bookmarking platform.

## History

Pinterest was founded in 2009 by Ben Silbermann, Paul Sciarra, and Evan Sharp. Silbermann had previously worked at Google. The platform launched in March 2010 and went public in April 2019.

## Leadership

- Bill Ready, Chief Executive Officer (since 2022)
- Ben Silbermann, co-founder and Executive Chairman
- Julia Brau Donnelly, Chief Financial Officer

## Platform

Pinterest allows users to:

### Pins
Save and share images and videos (Pins) to themed boards.

### Boards
Collections of Pins organized by topic or interest.

### Visual Search
Search using images to find similar content.

### Shopping
Discover and purchase products directly through the platform.

### Creator Tools
Tools for content creators to grow their audience.

## Business Model

Pinterest generates revenue primarily through advertising. The platform is particularly valuable for advertisers because users often have high purchase intent when searching for ideas.

## Use Cases

Popular categories include:
- Home decor and DIY
- Fashion and beauty
- Recipes and cooking
- Wedding planning
- Travel inspiration

## Competition

Pinterest competes with Instagram, TikTok, and Google in visual search and inspiration. The platform has a unique position as users often visit with specific intent rather than passive scrolling.

## Demographics

Pinterest has a predominantly female user base, though male users have been growing.
