# Spotify Technology S.A.

Spotify Technology S.A. is a Swedish-Luxembourgish audio streaming and media services provider. The company is one of the world's largest music streaming platforms with over 600 million monthly active users.

## History

Spotify was founded in 2006 in Stockholm, Sweden, by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon. The service launched in 2008 in select European countries and expanded to the United States in 2011.

## Leadership

- Daniel Ek, Chief Executive Officer and Chairman (co-founder)
- Paul Vogel, Chief Financial Officer
- Gustav Söderström, Co-President
- Alex Norström, Co-President

Martin Lorentzon serves as Vice Chairman.

## Service

Spotify offers music streaming, podcast streaming, and audiobook content. The platform features:

- Over 100 million tracks
- 5 million podcast titles
- Personalized playlists and recommendations
- Spotify Wrapped annual listening summary

## Business Model

Spotify operates two tiers:

- Spotify Free (ad-supported)
- Spotify Premium (subscription, ad-free with additional features)

The company has over 230 million premium subscribers.

## Podcasts

Spotify has invested heavily in podcasting:

- Acquired Gimlet Media (2019)
- Acquired Anchor (2019)
- Acquired The Ringer (2020)
- Exclusive deals with creators like Joe Rogan

## Competition

Spotify competes with:

- Apple Music
- Amazon Music
- YouTube Music
- Tidal
- Deezer
- Pandora (SiriusXM)

## Technology

Spotify's technology includes advanced recommendation algorithms, the Spotify for Developers API, and the Backstage developer portal (open-sourced).

## Headquarters

While incorporated in Luxembourg, Spotify maintains its main operational headquarters in Stockholm, Sweden, with major offices in New York, London, and other cities worldwide.
