# Greylock Partners

Greylock Partners is a venture capital firm headquartered in Menlo Park, California, with additional offices in San Francisco. One of the oldest VC firms in Silicon Valley.

## History

Founded in 1965 by Bill Elfers and Dan Gregory, making it one of the earliest venture capital firms. Originally based in Boston before expanding to Silicon Valley.

## Leadership

- Reid Hoffman, Partner (LinkedIn co-founder)
- David Sze, Partner
- Sarah Guo, Partner (now founder of Conviction)
- Seth Rosenberg, Partner

## Reid Hoffman

Reid Hoffman, co-founder of LinkedIn, joined Greylock after LinkedIn's acquisition by Microsoft, bringing significant expertise in network effects and scaling.

## Investment Philosophy

Greylock focuses on building long-term relationships with founders and is known for its operator-investor model, with many partners having entrepreneurial backgrounds.

## Podcast

Greylock produces "Greymatter," a podcast featuring conversations with founders and technologists about building companies.
