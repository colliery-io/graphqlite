# Slack Technologies

Slack Technologies, LLC is an American software company that developed the Slack workplace communication platform. The company is now a subsidiary of Salesforce following its 2021 acquisition.

## History

Slack was founded in 2009 by Stewart Butterfield, Eric Costello, Cal Henderson, and Serguei Mourachov. The team had previously created the game Glitch, and Slack emerged from the internal communication tools they built for game development.

## Leadership

- Denise Dresser, Chief Executive Officer (since 2024)
- Stewart Butterfield, co-founder and former CEO
- Cal Henderson, co-founder and Chief Technology Officer

## Platform

Slack provides a messaging platform for teams with:

### Features
- Channels (organized conversations)
- Direct messages
- File sharing
- Voice and video calls (Huddles)
- Workflow automation
- App integrations (thousands available)
- Slack Connect (inter-company collaboration)

### Slack AI
AI-powered features including search, conversation summaries, and intelligent suggestions.

## Salesforce Acquisition

Salesforce acquired Slack in July 2021 for approximately $27.7 billion. The acquisition aimed to strengthen Salesforce's position against Microsoft, which offers Teams as part of Microsoft 365.

## Competition

Slack competes primarily with:
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Workspace (Chat)
- Discord (for some use cases)
- Zoom Team Chat

## Usage

Slack has millions of daily active users across organizations of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies.
