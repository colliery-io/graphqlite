# PayPal Holdings

PayPal Holdings, Inc. is an American multinational financial technology company headquartered in San Jose, California. PayPal operates an online payments system that supports online money transfers.

## History

PayPal was founded in December 1998 as Confinity by Max Levchin, Peter Thiel, and Luke Nosek. It merged with X.com, Elon Musk's online banking company, in March 2000. eBay acquired PayPal in 2002 for $1.5 billion, and it was spun off as an independent company in 2015.

## Leadership

- Alex Chriss, President and Chief Executive Officer (since 2023)
- Dan Schulman, former CEO (2014-2023)
- Jamie Miller, Chief Financial Officer

## Products

### PayPal
Core payment platform for online transactions and peer-to-peer payments.

### Venmo
Mobile payment service popular among younger users for P2P payments.

### Braintree
Payment gateway for e-commerce businesses.

### Xoom
International money transfer service.

### Honey
Browser extension for finding coupons and deals (acquired 2020).

### PayPal Credit
Buy-now-pay-later financing options.

## PayPal Mafia

PayPal is famous for its early employees, known as the "PayPal Mafia," who went on to found or lead major tech companies including Tesla, LinkedIn, YouTube, Yelp, and Palantir.

## Competition

PayPal competes with Stripe, Square (Block), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and traditional credit card companies.

## Cryptocurrency

PayPal enabled cryptocurrency buying, selling, and holding in 2020, and launched its own stablecoin, PayPal USD (PYUSD), in 2023.
