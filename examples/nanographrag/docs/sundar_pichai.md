# Sundar Pichai

Pichai Sundararajan, known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-American business executive. He is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google.

## Early Life

Sundar Pichai was born on June 10, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. He earned a degree in metallurgical engineering from IIT Kharagpur, an M.S. from Stanford University, and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

## Career

### Joining Google
Pichai joined Google in 2004 and worked on the Google Toolbar before leading product development for Google Chrome.

### Key Products
Pichai led the development of several major Google products:

- Google Chrome browser (launched 2008)
- Chrome OS (launched 2011)
- Google Drive
- Android (became head of Android in 2013)

### Google CEO
Pichai became CEO of Google in August 2015, when the company was restructured under the new parent company Alphabet. He was named CEO of Alphabet as well in December 2019, when Larry Page and Sergey Brin stepped back from day-to-day operations.

### Major Initiatives as CEO
- Expansion of Google Cloud to compete with AWS and Azure
- Development of Google's AI capabilities, including Bard and Gemini
- Merger of DeepMind and Google Brain into Google DeepMind
- Navigation of antitrust challenges in multiple jurisdictions

## Leadership Style

Pichai is known for his calm demeanor and consensus-building approach. He has emphasized AI development as Google's primary strategic focus.

## Compensation

Pichai is one of the highest-paid executives in the world, with compensation packages that have exceeded $200 million in some years.

## Personal Life

Pichai is married to Anjali Pichai, whom he met at IIT Kharagpur. They have two children and reside in the San Francisco Bay Area.
