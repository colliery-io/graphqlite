# International Business Machines Corporation (IBM)

IBM is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Armonk, New York. One of the oldest and largest technology companies in the world.

## History

IBM traces its origins to 1911 when the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR) was formed. The company was renamed IBM in 1924 under Thomas Watson Sr.

## Leadership

- Arvind Krishna, Chairman and CEO (since 2020)
- Previous notable CEOs: Ginni Rometty, Sam Palmisano, Lou Gerstner

## Business Segments

### Hybrid Cloud
- Red Hat (acquired 2019 for $34 billion)
- IBM Cloud
- Cloud Pak solutions

### AI and Data
- Watson AI platform
- Data and AI software

### Consulting
- IBM Consulting (formerly Global Business Services)

### Infrastructure
- Mainframes (z Systems)
- Storage solutions

## Historical Impact

### Computing Milestones
- IBM PC (1981) established the PC standard
- System/360 mainframes
- FORTRAN programming language
- Relational databases

### Watson
IBM Watson famously won Jeopardy! in 2011, demonstrating AI capabilities.

## Red Hat Acquisition

The 2019 acquisition of Red Hat for $34 billion was IBM's largest acquisition and signaled its pivot to hybrid cloud.

## Research

IBM Research has produced numerous innovations, including multiple Nobel Prize winners and pioneering work in quantum computing.
