# ServiceNow, Inc.

ServiceNow is an American software company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. The company provides cloud-based workflow automation and IT service management.

## History

Founded in 2004 by Fred Luddy in San Diego. The company initially focused on IT service management before expanding to enterprise workflow automation.

## Leadership

- Bill McDermott, Chairman and CEO (since 2019)
- Fred Luddy, Founder and Board member
- Previous CEO: John Donahoe (now Nike CEO)

## Platform

### Now Platform
ServiceNow's core platform enables enterprises to digitize workflows across:

- IT Service Management (ITSM)
- IT Operations Management (ITOM)
- Customer Service Management
- HR Service Delivery
- Security Operations
- Field Service Management

## Key Capabilities

### Workflow Automation
Low-code/no-code workflow builder for business processes.

### AI/ML
Predictive intelligence and virtual agents powered by machine learning.

### Integration
IntegrationHub connects ServiceNow with other enterprise systems.

## Market Position

ServiceNow is the dominant player in IT service management and is expanding into broader enterprise workflow automation.

## Growth

The company has achieved over $8 billion in annual revenue with consistent 20%+ growth, making it one of the fastest-growing enterprise software companies.

## Customer Base

ServiceNow serves approximately 85% of Fortune 500 companies.
