# Coinbase Global

Coinbase Global, Inc. is an American cryptocurrency exchange platform headquartered in San Francisco, California, though it operates as a remote-first company. Coinbase is the largest cryptocurrency exchange in the United States.

## History

Coinbase was founded in June 2012 by Brian Armstrong and Fred Ehrsam. Armstrong, a former Airbnb engineer, started the company from a small apartment. The company went public through a direct listing on NASDAQ in April 2021.

## Leadership

- Brian Armstrong, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Alesia Haas, Chief Financial Officer
- Emilie Choi, President and Chief Operating Officer

Fred Ehrsam left Coinbase in 2017 and co-founded Paradigm, a crypto investment firm.

## Services

### Coinbase (Consumer)
A user-friendly platform for buying, selling, and storing cryptocurrencies.

### Coinbase Pro (Advanced Trading)
A trading platform for more sophisticated traders with advanced charting and order types.

### Coinbase Prime
Institutional trading and custody services for large investors and businesses.

### Coinbase Wallet
A self-custody wallet that gives users control of their private keys.

### Base
A Layer 2 blockchain network built on Ethereum, launched in 2023.

## SEC Legal Issues

Coinbase has faced legal action from the SEC regarding the classification of certain cryptocurrencies as securities. The company has been vocal in calling for clearer crypto regulations.

## Competition

Coinbase competes with Binance, Kraken, Gemini, and traditional brokerages like Robinhood that offer crypto trading.
