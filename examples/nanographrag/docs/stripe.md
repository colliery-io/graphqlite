# Stripe, Inc.

Stripe, Inc. is an Irish-American financial services and software-as-a-service company headquartered in San Francisco, California, and Dublin, Ireland. Stripe provides payment processing software and APIs for e-commerce websites and mobile applications.

## History

Stripe was founded in 2010 by brothers Patrick Collison and John Collison, who grew up in Dromineer, Ireland. The company launched publicly in September 2011 after participating in Y Combinator.

## Leadership

- Patrick Collison, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- John Collison, President and co-founder
- David Singleton, Chief Technology Officer
- Dhivya Suryadevara, Chief Financial Officer

## Products

Stripe offers a suite of financial infrastructure products:

### Payments
- Stripe Payments (core payment processing)
- Stripe Checkout (pre-built payment pages)
- Stripe Elements (customizable payment UI)

### Financial Services
- Stripe Connect (marketplace payments)
- Stripe Billing (subscription management)
- Stripe Atlas (company incorporation)
- Stripe Capital (business financing)
- Stripe Treasury (banking-as-a-service)
- Stripe Issuing (card issuance)

## Customers

Stripe processes payments for millions of businesses, including:

- Amazon
- Google
- Shopify
- Instacart
- Salesforce
- Zoom

## Competition

Stripe competes with:

- PayPal and Braintree
- Block (formerly Square)
- Adyen
- Worldpay

## Valuation

Stripe was valued at $95 billion in 2021, making it one of the most valuable private companies in the world, before a reduced valuation in subsequent funding rounds.

## Press (Stripe's Publishing Arm)

Stripe Press publishes books about economic and technological progress, including works on infrastructure, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
