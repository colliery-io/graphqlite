# Andreessen Horowitz (a16z)

Andreessen Horowitz, known as a16z, is an American venture capital firm headquartered in Menlo Park, California. The firm focuses on technology investments across multiple sectors.

## History

Founded in 2009 by Marc Andreessen and Ben Horowitz. Marc Andreessen co-created Mosaic and founded Netscape. Ben Horowitz was CEO of Opsware, which sold to HP for $1.6 billion.

## Leadership

- Marc Andreessen, Co-founder and General Partner
- Ben Horowitz, Co-founder and General Partner
- Martin Casado, General Partner
- Chris Dixon, General Partner (crypto focus)

## Platform Model

a16z pioneered the "platform" model in venture capital, providing portfolio companies with operational support including recruiting, marketing, and business development resources.

## Crypto Focus

a16z crypto is one of the largest crypto-focused venture funds, having raised multiple billion-dollar funds dedicated to blockchain and Web3 investments.

## Media

The firm runs Future, a media property covering technology trends and ideas.
