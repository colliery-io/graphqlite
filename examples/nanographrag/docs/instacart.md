# Instacart

Instacart (Maplebear Inc.) is an American retail company that operates a grocery delivery and pick-up service in the United States and Canada. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, California.

## History

Instacart was founded in 2012 by Apoorva Mehta, a former Amazon engineer, along with Max Mullen and Brandon Leonardo. The company went public in September 2023.

## Leadership

- Fidji Simo, Chief Executive Officer (since 2021)
- Apoorva Mehta, founder and Executive Chairman
- Emily Reuter, Chief Financial Officer

## Services

### Instacart Delivery
Same-day delivery from grocery stores and retailers.

### Instacart Pickup
Curbside pickup from partner stores.

### Instacart for Business
Grocery delivery for companies and organizations.

### Instacart Ads
Advertising platform for consumer packaged goods brands.

### Caper Cart
Smart shopping cart technology acquired in 2021.

## Shopper Network

Instacart uses independent contractors called "shoppers" who select items at stores and deliver them to customers.

## Retail Partners

Instacart partners with major retailers including:
- Costco
- Kroger
- Albertsons
- Publix
- Wegmans
- Sam's Club

## Competition

Instacart competes with:
- Amazon Fresh
- Walmart Delivery
- DoorDash
- Shipt (Target)

## COVID-19 Impact

The pandemic dramatically increased demand for Instacart's services as consumers sought contactless grocery shopping options.
