# HP Inc.

HP Inc. is an American multinational information technology company headquartered in Palo Alto, California. The company focuses on personal computers and printing.

## History

The original Hewlett-Packard was founded in 1939 by Bill Hewlett and Dave Packard in a Palo Alto garage, often cited as the birthplace of Silicon Valley.

## HP Split (2015)

In 2015, Hewlett-Packard split into two companies:
- **HP Inc.** - PCs and printers (NYSE: HPQ)
- **Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE)** - Enterprise servers, storage, networking

## Leadership

- Enrique Lores, President and CEO
- Previous CEOs: Dion Weisler, Meg Whitman (pre-split)

## Products

### Personal Systems
- Consumer PCs (Pavilion, Envy, Spectre)
- Business PCs (ProBook, EliteBook)
- Gaming (OMEN)
- Workstations (Z series)

### Printing
- Consumer printers
- Enterprise printing
- 3D printing
- Managed print services

## Market Position

HP is one of the world's largest PC manufacturers, competing with Lenovo and Dell for market leadership.

## Instant Ink

HP's subscription printing service represents a shift toward recurring revenue models.

## 3D Printing

HP has invested significantly in industrial 3D printing technology (Multi Jet Fusion) for manufacturing applications.
