# CrowdStrike Holdings

CrowdStrike Holdings, Inc. is an American cybersecurity technology company headquartered in Austin, Texas. The company provides cloud-native endpoint security, threat intelligence, and cyberattack response services.

## History

CrowdStrike was founded in 2011 by George Kurtz, Dmitri Alperovitch, and Gregg Marston. George Kurtz previously served as CTO of McAfee. The company went public in June 2019.

## Leadership

- George Kurtz, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Burt Podbere, Chief Financial Officer
- Michael Sentonas, President

Dmitri Alperovitch, a co-founder, left the company in 2020 to focus on policy work.

## Products

### Falcon Platform
CrowdStrike's cloud-native Falcon platform provides:

- Endpoint detection and response (EDR)
- Next-generation antivirus
- Threat intelligence
- IT hygiene
- Identity protection
- Cloud security

### Notable Investigations
CrowdStrike has been involved in investigating major cyber incidents, including the 2016 Democratic National Committee hack and various nation-state cyber operations.

## Competition

CrowdStrike competes with:

- Microsoft (Defender)
- Palo Alto Networks
- SentinelOne
- Carbon Black (VMware)
- Symantec (Broadcom)

## Market Position

CrowdStrike is one of the leading endpoint security vendors and has grown rapidly by displacing traditional antivirus solutions with its cloud-native approach.

## July 2024 Incident

A faulty software update in July 2024 caused widespread outages affecting millions of Windows computers globally, demonstrating both the company's reach and the risks of security software.
