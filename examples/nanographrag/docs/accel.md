# Accel

Accel is a global venture capital firm with offices in Palo Alto, London, and Bangalore. The firm focuses on early and growth-stage technology investments.

## History

Founded in 1983 by Arthur Patterson and Jim Swartz. Accel has been one of the most successful early-stage investors in enterprise software and consumer technology.

## Facebook Investment

Accel's $12.7 million investment in Facebook in 2005, led by Jim Breyer, is one of the most successful venture investments ever, returning billions.

## Global Presence

Accel operates dedicated funds for Europe and India, with local partners who understand regional markets.

## Investment Approach

Known for long-term partnerships with founders and expertise in both consumer and enterprise software companies.
