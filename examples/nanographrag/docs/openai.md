# OpenAI

OpenAI is an American artificial intelligence research organization headquartered in San Francisco, California. The company develops artificial intelligence systems including large language models and has created some of the most advanced AI systems in the world.

## History

OpenAI was founded in December 2015 as a non-profit research organization. Co-founders included Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Elon Musk, Jessica Livingston, and Peter Thiel. Elon Musk departed the board in 2018.

In 2019, OpenAI transitioned to a "capped-profit" structure, creating OpenAI LP to attract investment while maintaining its mission.

## Leadership

Sam Altman serves as Chief Executive Officer. In November 2023, the board briefly fired Altman before reinstating him days later following employee protests and investor pressure. Other key executives include:

- Greg Brockman, President (briefly departed during the November 2023 incident)
- Mira Murati, Chief Technology Officer
- Brad Lightcap, Chief Operating Officer

## Products

OpenAI has developed several breakthrough AI systems:

- GPT-3 (2020) and GPT-4 (2023) language models
- ChatGPT conversational AI (launched November 2022)
- DALL-E image generation models
- Whisper speech recognition
- Codex programming assistant

## Microsoft Partnership

Microsoft has invested over $13 billion in OpenAI, making it the company's largest investor and exclusive cloud provider. OpenAI technology powers Microsoft's Copilot products and Azure OpenAI Service.

## Competition

OpenAI competes with Anthropic in AI safety and large language models, with Google DeepMind in AI research, with Meta AI in open-source models, and with various startups in AI applications.

## Research

OpenAI has published influential research on reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), scaling laws for neural networks, and AI safety techniques.
