# Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE)

Hewlett Packard Enterprise is an American multinational enterprise information technology company headquartered in Spring, Texas.

## History

HPE was created in 2015 when Hewlett-Packard split into two companies. HPE retained the enterprise-focused businesses.

## Leadership

- Antonio Neri, President and CEO
- Previous: Meg Whitman (interim during split)

## Business Segments

### Compute
- ProLiant servers
- Synergy composable infrastructure
- High-performance computing (Cray)

### Storage
- Primera
- Nimble Storage
- Alletra

### Intelligent Edge
- Aruba networking
- Edge computing solutions

### Financial Services
- HPE Financial Services

## GreenLake

HPE GreenLake is the company's as-a-service platform, offering pay-per-use consumption models for on-premises infrastructure.

## Acquisitions

### Notable Acquisitions
- Aruba Networks (2015, $3 billion)
- SGI/Cray (2019, $1.3 billion)
- Juniper Networks (2024, $14 billion announced)

## Spin-offs

HPE has divested several businesses:
- Software segment (to Micro Focus)
- Enterprise Services (merged with CSC to form DXC)

## Supercomputing

HPE's Cray division builds some of the world's most powerful supercomputers, including systems for government and research institutions.
