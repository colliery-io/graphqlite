# Workday, Inc.

Workday is an American enterprise cloud software company headquartered in Pleasanton, California. The company provides financial management and human capital management applications.

## History

Founded in 2005 by Dave Duffield and Aneel Bhusri. Duffield had previously founded PeopleSoft, which was acquired by Oracle in a hostile takeover.

## Leadership

- Carl Eschenbach, CEO (since 2024)
- Aneel Bhusri, Co-founder and Executive Chairman
- Dave Duffield, Co-founder

## Products

### Human Capital Management (HCM)
- Core HR
- Talent management
- Payroll
- Workforce planning
- Learning management

### Financial Management
- General ledger
- Accounts payable/receivable
- Revenue management
- Procurement

### Planning
- Adaptive Planning (acquired 2018)
- Budgeting and forecasting

## Technology

### Cloud-Native
Workday was built cloud-native, unlike competitors who migrated legacy systems.

### In-Memory Architecture
Uses in-memory computing for real-time analytics.

## Competition

Workday competes primarily with Oracle, SAP, and ADP in the enterprise HR and finance software market.

## Customer Base

Workday serves over 10,000 customers, including more than 50% of Fortune 500 companies for HCM.

## PeopleSoft Connection

Many Workday features and design philosophies trace back to PeopleSoft, as the founders brought their experience building enterprise applications.
