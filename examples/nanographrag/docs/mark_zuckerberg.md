# Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg is an American businessman and philanthropist. He is the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Meta Platforms, Inc., the company that owns Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and other products.

## Early Life

Mark Zuckerberg was born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York. He developed an interest in programming as a child and created several software programs before attending Harvard University.

## Career

### Facebook
Zuckerberg launched Facebook from his Harvard dormitory room on February 4, 2004, along with fellow students Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes. The platform quickly expanded from Harvard to other universities and then to the general public.

### Meta

### Acquisitions
Under Zuckerberg's leadership, Facebook acquired:

- Instagram (2012) for $1 billion
- WhatsApp (2014) for $19 billion
- Oculus VR (2014) for $2 billion

## Leadership Style

Zuckerberg maintains controlling voting power in Meta through a dual-class share structure, allowing him to make major decisions despite not holding majority ownership.

## Wealth

Zuckerberg is one of the wealthiest people in the world, with his fortune primarily tied to his stake in Meta Platforms.

## Philanthropy

In 2015, Zuckerberg and his wife Priscilla Chan founded the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, pledging to give away 99% of their Facebook shares (worth about $45 billion at the time) during their lifetimes.

## Personal Life

Zuckerberg is married to Priscilla Chan, whom he met at Harvard. They have three daughters together.
