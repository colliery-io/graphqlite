# Palantir Technologies

Palantir Technologies Inc. is an American software company specializing in big data analytics. The company is headquartered in Denver, Colorado, and is known for its work with government agencies and large enterprises.

## History

Palantir was founded in 2003 by Peter Thiel, Alex Karp, Joe Lonsdale, Stephen Cohen, and Nathan Gettings. The company was initially funded by In-Q-Tel, the CIA's venture capital arm, and named after the seeing stones in J.R.R. Tolkien's Lord of the Rings.

## Leadership

- Alex Karp, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Stephen Cohen, President and co-founder
- Shyam Sankar, Chief Technology Officer

Peter Thiel serves as Chairman of the Board.

## Products

### Palantir Gotham
A platform designed for government and defense agencies, used for counterterrorism analysis, fraud detection, and intelligence operations.

### Palantir Foundry
An enterprise data platform for commercial customers, enabling data integration, analysis, and operational decision-making.

### Palantir Apollo
A continuous delivery system that manages and deploys Palantir's software across various environments.

### Palantir AIP
Artificial Intelligence Platform that integrates large language models with Palantir's existing platforms.

## Customers

Palantir serves government agencies including the CIA, NSA, FBI, and various military branches. Commercial customers include Airbus, Ferrari, and major healthcare organizations.

## Competition

Palantir competes with companies like Snowflake, Databricks, and traditional enterprise software providers like IBM and SAP in the data analytics space.
