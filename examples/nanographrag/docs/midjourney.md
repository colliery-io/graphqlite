# Midjourney

Midjourney is an independent research lab that produces an AI program that generates images from textual descriptions. The service operates primarily through Discord.

## History

Founded in 2021 by David Holz. Holz was a co-founder of Leap Motion, a hand-tracking technology company. Midjourney launched publicly in July 2022.

## Leadership

- David Holz, Founder and CEO

## Product

### Image Generation
- Text-to-image AI generation
- Distinctive artistic style
- High-quality outputs
- Various aspect ratios and styles

### Discord Interface
Unique among AI tools, Midjourney operates primarily through Discord:
- /imagine command for generation
- Community channels
- Image sharing and remixing

### Web Interface
Beta web interface for subscribers.

## Model Evolution

- V1-V3: Early versions
- V4: Significant quality improvement
- V5: Photorealistic capabilities
- V6: Current generation with enhanced prompting

## Subscription Tiers

- Basic

## Community

Midjourney has one of the largest AI art communities:
- Discord server with millions of members
- Shared creations and prompts
- Community guidelines

## Competition

Competes with Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, and Adobe Firefly in image generation.

## Controversies

Like other AI image generators, Midjourney faces debates about:
- Copyright and training data
- Artist compensation
- Deepfakes and misuse
