# Discord Inc.

Discord Inc. is an American company that operates Discord, a social platform originally designed for gamers that has expanded to serve diverse communities. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, California.

## History

Discord was founded in 2015 by Jason Citron and Stan Vishnevskiy. Citron had previously founded OpenFeint, a mobile gaming platform acquired by GREE. Discord launched publicly in May 2015 as a voice chat platform for gamers.

## Leadership

- Jason Citron, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Stan Vishnevskiy, Chief Technology Officer and co-founder
- Tomasz Marcinkowski, Chief Financial Officer

## Platform

Discord provides free voice, video, and text communication:

### Features
- Servers (community spaces)
- Voice channels
- Text channels
- Video calls and screen sharing
- Stage channels (for events)
- Forum channels
- Discord Nitro (premium subscription)

### Bot Ecosystem
Discord has a rich ecosystem of bots that add functionality like moderation, music, games, and integrations.

## User Base

Discord has over 150 million monthly active users and hosts millions of active servers. While originally gaming-focused, the platform now serves communities around education, content creation, and various interests.

## Failed Acquisitions

Microsoft reportedly attempted to acquire Discord for approximately $12 billion in 2021, but Discord decided to remain independent.

## Competition

Discord competes with Slack and Microsoft Teams in communication, and with platforms like Reddit and Facebook Groups for community building.

## Monetization

Discord generates revenue through Nitro subscriptions, server boosts, and merchandise, rather than advertising.
