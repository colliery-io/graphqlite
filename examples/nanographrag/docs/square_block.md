# Block, Inc.

Block, Inc. (formerly Square, Inc.) is an American financial services and digital payments company headquartered in San Francisco, California. The company was renamed from Square to Block in December 2021.

## History

Square was founded in 2009 by Jack Dorsey and Jim McKelvey. The company launched its first product, a small card reader that plugged into smartphones, in 2010. Dorsey had previously co-founded Twitter.

## Leadership

- Jack Dorsey, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Amrita Ahuja, Chief Financial Officer
- Jim McKelvey, co-founder and Board member

## Products and Companies

### Square (Seller Ecosystem)
- Square Reader (card readers)
- Square Terminal
- Square Register
- Square Online (e-commerce)
- Square Payroll
- Square Invoices

### Cash App
A peer-to-peer payment app that also offers:
- Stock and Bitcoin trading
- Cash Card (debit card)
- Direct deposit
- Tax filing

### TIDAL
Music streaming service acquired in 2021 from Jay-Z and other artists.

### TBD
A subsidiary focused on building decentralized financial infrastructure.

### Spiral
A subsidiary dedicated to Bitcoin development.

## Afterpay Acquisition

Block acquired Afterpay, an Australian buy-now-pay-later company, for $29 billion in 2022.

## Bitcoin Focus

Jack Dorsey has been a prominent Bitcoin advocate, and Block has significant investments in Bitcoin and blockchain technology.

## Competition

Block competes with PayPal, Stripe, Shopify, and traditional payment processors in merchant services, and with Venmo, Zelle, and PayPal in P2P payments.
