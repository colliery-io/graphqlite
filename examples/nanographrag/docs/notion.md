# Notion Labs, Inc.

Notion is a productivity and note-taking application that combines notes, databases, kanban boards, wikis, and calendars. The company is headquartered in San Francisco.

## History

Founded in 2013 by Ivan Zhao and Simon Last. After initial struggles, the company nearly shut down before relaunching successfully in 2018.

## Leadership

- Ivan Zhao, Co-founder and CEO
- Simon Last, Co-founder
- Akshay Kothari, COO (former LinkedIn executive)

## Product Features

### Core Capabilities
- Rich text documents and notes
- Databases with multiple views (table, board, calendar, gallery)
- Wikis and documentation
- Project management
- Templates

### Notion AI
AI writing assistant integrated into the platform.

### Notion Calendar
Calendar application (acquired Cron in 2022).

## Technology

### Block-Based Editor
Everything in Notion is a "block" that can be nested, referenced, and transformed.

### Databases
Flexible databases that can display as tables, boards, timelines, or other views.

## Business Model

Freemium model with paid tiers for teams and enterprises.

## Growth

Notion grew significantly during the pandemic as remote teams sought collaborative tools. The company reached $10 billion valuation.

## Community

Active community sharing templates, workflows, and Notion-based products.
