# Scale AI

Scale AI is an American data labeling and AI infrastructure company. The company provides training data for AI systems across various industries.

## History

Founded in 2016 by Alexandr Wang and Lucy Guo. Wang was 19 when he founded Scale, having previously worked at Quora. Scale became one of the youngest companies to reach unicorn status.

## Leadership

- Alexandr Wang, Co-founder and CEO
- Lucy Guo, Co-founder (departed to start other ventures)

## Products

### Data Labeling
- Image annotation
- Video annotation
- Text annotation
- 3D/LiDAR labeling
- Audio transcription

### Scale GenAI Platform
- RLHF data for large language models
- Evaluation and testing
- Model fine-tuning data

### Scale Donovan
AI assistant for defense and government applications.

### Scale EGP (Enterprise Generative AI Platform)
Enterprise AI deployment and management.

## Customers

### Industries
- Autonomous vehicles
- Defense and government
- E-commerce

- Healthcare

### Notable Customers
- OpenAI
- Meta
- Microsoft
- General Motors
- US Department of Defense

## RLHF Expertise

Scale became crucial to LLM development by providing RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) data for major AI companies.

## Defense Work

Scale has significant government contracts, including work with the US military on AI applications.

## Valuation

Reached $7.3 billion valuation, making Alexandr Wang one of the youngest self-made billionaires.
