# Google LLC

Google LLC is an American multinational technology company focusing on artificial intelligence, online advertising, search engine technology, cloud computing, computer software, and consumer electronics. The company is headquartered at the Googleplex in Mountain View, California.

## History

Google was founded on September 4, 1998, by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were Ph.D. students at Stanford University in California. The company went public in August 2004 with an initial public offering.

## Leadership

Sundar Pichai has served as Chief Executive Officer of Google since 2015 and of parent company Alphabet Inc. since 2019. Larry Page and Sergey Brin remain as controlling shareholders and board members of Alphabet.

## Parent Company

In October 2015, Google was reorganized as a subsidiary of Alphabet Inc. Other Alphabet subsidiaries include:

- Waymo (autonomous vehicles)
- Verily (life sciences)
- DeepMind (artificial intelligence research)
- Calico (biotechnology)
- Google Fiber (internet service)

## Products and Services

Google operates the world's most popular search engine. Other major products include:

- YouTube (acquired in 2006)
- Android operating system
- Google Cloud Platform
- Google Workspace (Gmail, Google Docs, etc.)
- Google Maps
- Chrome web browser

## Competition

Google competes with Microsoft in cloud computing through Azure and in productivity software through Microsoft 365. In digital advertising, Meta (Facebook) is a major competitor. Amazon Web Services leads the cloud infrastructure market ahead of Google Cloud.

## Acquisitions

Notable acquisitions include YouTube (2006), DoubleClick (2007), Motorola Mobility (2012, later sold to Lenovo), Nest Labs (2014), and Fitbit (2021).
