# Kleiner Perkins

Kleiner Perkins is an American venture capital firm headquartered in Menlo Park, California. One of the oldest and most prestigious VC firms in Silicon Valley.

## History

Founded in 1972 by Eugene Kleiner and Tom Perkins. Eugene Kleiner was one of the "traitorous eight" who founded Fairchild Semiconductor.

## John Doerr

John Doerr joined in 1980 and led investments in Google, Amazon, and Netscape. He authored "Measure What Matters" about OKRs.

## Clean Tech

Kleiner Perkins famously invested heavily in clean technology in the 2000s, with mixed results, leading to a strategic refocus.

## Legacy

KPCB (Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, the original name) helped establish the model of venture capital in Silicon Valley.
