# DoorDash

DoorDash, Inc. is an American food delivery platform headquartered in San Francisco, California. The company is the largest food delivery service in the United States by market share.

## History

DoorDash was founded in 2013 by Tony Xu, Stanley Tang, Andy Fang, and Evan Moore while they were students at Stanford University. The company went public in December 2020.

## Leadership

- Tony Xu, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Prabir Adarkar, Chief Financial Officer
- Christopher Payne, Chief Operating Officer

## Services

### DoorDash Marketplace
Core food delivery platform connecting customers with restaurants.

### DashPass
Subscription service offering free delivery and reduced fees for members.

### DoorDash Drive
White-label delivery service for restaurants and retailers.

### Wolt
European food and retail delivery platform acquired in 2022 for $8.1 billion.

### Grocery and Retail Delivery
Expansion beyond restaurants to grocery stores, convenience stores, and retail.

## Dasher Network

DoorDash relies on independent contractors called "Dashers" to perform deliveries. The company has faced legal challenges regarding worker classification.

## Competition

DoorDash competes with:
- Uber Eats
- Grubhub (owned by Just Eat Takeaway)
- Instacart (grocery)
- Amazon Fresh

## Market Position

DoorDash holds approximately 60% of the U.S. food delivery market, significantly ahead of Uber Eats and Grubhub.
