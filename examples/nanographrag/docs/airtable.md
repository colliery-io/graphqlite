# Airtable Inc.

Airtable is a cloud collaboration service headquartered in San Francisco. The platform combines spreadsheet and database functionality with a user-friendly interface.

## History

Founded in 2012 by Howie Liu, Andrew Ofstad, and Emmett Nicholas. The company launched publicly in 2015 after years of development.

## Leadership

- Howie Liu, Co-founder and CEO
- Andrew Ofstad, Co-founder and Head of Product
- Emmett Nicholas, Co-founder and CTO

## Product Features

### Bases
- Spreadsheet-like interface with database capabilities
- Multiple views (grid, calendar, kanban, gallery, timeline)
- Linked records and relationships
- Formulas and computed fields

### Automations
Workflow automation without code.

### Interface Designer
Custom app interfaces built on Airtable data.

### Extensions
Third-party and built-in extensions for additional functionality.

## Use Cases

- Project management
- Content calendars
- CRM
- Inventory tracking
- Event planning
- Research databases

## Business Model

Freemium with paid tiers:
- Free (limited records)
- Team
- Business
- Enterprise

## Integrations

Extensive integrations with tools like Slack, Zapier, and various business applications.

## Competition

Airtable competes with Notion, Smartsheet, and traditional spreadsheet/database tools.

## Funding

Raised over $1.4 billion with a peak valuation of $11 billion.
