# Snap Inc.

Snap Inc. is an American technology company headquartered in Santa Monica, California. The company is best known for Snapchat, a multimedia messaging app.

## History

Snapchat was created by Evan Spiegel, Bobby Murphy, and Reggie Brown as a project at Stanford University in 2011. The app launched in September 2011 and gained popularity for its disappearing messages. Snap Inc. went public in March 2017.

## Leadership

- Evan Spiegel, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Bobby Murphy, Chief Technology Officer and co-founder
- Derek Andersen, Chief Financial Officer

Reggie Brown, who claimed to have conceived the idea, settled a lawsuit with the company in 2014.

## Products

### Snapchat
Core messaging app featuring:
- Snaps (disappearing photos and videos)
- Stories (24-hour content)
- Chat messaging
- Discover (publisher content)
- Spotlight (short-form video feed)
- Snap Map (location sharing)

### Spectacles
Augmented reality glasses for capturing content.

### Lens Studio
Platform for creating AR lenses and filters.

### Snapchat+
Premium subscription with exclusive features.

## Augmented Reality

Snap has invested heavily in AR technology, with millions of AR lenses created and billions of daily AR engagements.

## Competition

Snap competes with TikTok, Instagram, and other social media platforms. Instagram's copying of Stories significantly impacted Snapchat's growth.

## Demographics

Snapchat is particularly popular among younger users, with strong penetration in the 13-34 age demographic.
