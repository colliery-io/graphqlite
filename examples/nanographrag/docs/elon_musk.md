# Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk is a businessman, investor, and entrepreneur. He is the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of SpaceX; CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; owner and CTO of X Corp. (formerly Twitter); founder of The Boring Company; co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI; and president of the Musk Foundation.

## Early Life

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, to Maye Musk (a model and dietitian) and Errol Musk (an engineer). He moved to Canada at age 17 and later to the United States to attend the University of Pennsylvania.

## Career

### Early Ventures
- Zip2: Co-founded in 1996, sold to Compaq for $307 million in 1999
- X.com: Founded in 1999, merged with Confinity to become PayPal, sold to eBay for $1.5 billion in 2002

### SpaceX
Founded Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX) in 2002 with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling Mars colonization. Under his leadership, SpaceX developed the Falcon 1, Falcon 9, Falcon Heavy, and Starship rockets.

### Tesla
Joined Tesla Motors in 2004 as chairman and lead investor. Became CEO in 2008. Under his leadership, Tesla became the world's most valuable automaker and accelerated the transition to electric vehicles.

### Other Ventures
- The Boring Company (2016): Infrastructure and tunnel construction
- Neuralink (2016): Brain-computer interfaces
- OpenAI (2015): Co-founded, departed board in 2018
- X Corp. (2022): Acquired Twitter for $44 billion

## Personal Life

Musk has had children with several partners and has been known for his active presence on social media, particularly on X (formerly Twitter).

## Wealth

Musk has been ranked as the world's wealthiest person at various times, with his wealth primarily derived from his stakes in Tesla and SpaceX.
