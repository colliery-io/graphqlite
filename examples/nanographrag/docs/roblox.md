# Roblox Corporation

Roblox Corporation is an American video game developer and platform company headquartered in San Mateo, California. The company operates Roblox, a platform that allows users to create and play games made by other users.

## History

Roblox was founded in 2004 by David Baszucki and Erik Cassel. The platform launched in 2006 as DynaBlocks before being renamed to Roblox. The company went public through a direct listing in March 2021.

## Leadership

- David Baszucki, Chief Executive Officer and co-founder
- Michael Guthrie, Chief Financial Officer
- Manuel Bronstein, Chief Product Officer

Erik Cassel, co-founder, passed away in 2013.

## Platform

Roblox is a platform where users can:

- Play millions of user-created games ("experiences")
- Create games using Roblox Studio
- Socialize with other users
- Purchase virtual items with Robux currency
- Attend virtual events and concerts

### Roblox Studio
A free development tool that allows creators to build 3D experiences using Lua scripting language.

### Economy
Creators can monetize their games through Robux, which can be converted to real currency through the Developer Exchange program.

## User Demographics

Roblox is particularly popular among younger users, with a significant portion of users under 16 years old. The platform has over 70 million daily active users.

## Competition

Roblox competes with Minecraft (Microsoft), Fortnite (Epic Games), and various metaverse platforms.

## Metaverse Vision

Roblox positions itself as a metaverse company, hosting virtual concerts, brand experiences, and social gatherings.
