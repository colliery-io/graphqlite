# Electronic Arts

Electronic Arts Inc. (EA) is an American video game company headquartered in Redwood City, California. EA is one of the largest video game publishers in the world.

## History

Electronic Arts was founded on May 27, 1982, by Trip Hawkins, a former Apple employee. The company pioneered treating game developers as artists, featuring their names prominently on game packaging.

## Leadership

- Andrew Wilson, Chief Executive Officer (since 2013)
- Chris Suh, Chief Financial Officer
- Laura Miele, President of EA Entertainment

## Studios

EA owns numerous development studios including:
- DICE (Battlefield)
- Respawn Entertainment (Apex Legends, Titanfall)
- Maxis (The Sims)
- BioWare (Mass Effect, Dragon Age)
- EA Sports
- Criterion Games

## Business Model

EA generates revenue through game sales, live services, and EA Play subscription service. The company has faced criticism for microtransactions and "loot boxes" in some games.

## Competition

EA competes with Activision Blizzard (Microsoft), Take-Two Interactive, Ubisoft, Sony, and various mobile game publishers.
