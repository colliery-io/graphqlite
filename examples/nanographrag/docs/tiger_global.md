# Tiger Global Management

Tiger Global Management is an American investment firm founded by Chase Coleman. The firm invests in both public and private technology companies.

## History

Founded in 2001 by Chase Coleman III, who had worked at Tiger Management under Julian Robertson. Tiger Global became one of the most aggressive technology investors.

## Leadership

- Chase Coleman III, Founder and Managing Partner
- Scott Shleifer, Partner (Private Equity)
- John Curtius, Partner (former, now Forge Point Capital)

## Investment Approach

### Public Markets
Long/short equity hedge fund focused on technology and emerging market companies.

### Private Markets
One of the largest and most active late-stage private investors, known for:
- Fast decision-making
- Large check sizes
- Minimal board involvement
- High valuations

## 2021 Activity

Tiger Global was extremely active in 2021, completing over 300 deals and deploying unprecedented capital into private technology companies.

## 2022 Challenges

The firm faced significant losses in 2022 as technology valuations declined, leading to more measured investment pace.
