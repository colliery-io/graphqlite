# Satya Nadella

Satya Narayana Nadella is an Indian-American business executive who serves as the chairman and chief executive officer of Microsoft. Under his leadership, Microsoft has undergone a significant transformation, embracing cloud computing and artificial intelligence.

## Early Life

Satya Nadella was born on August 19, 1967, in Hyderabad, India. He earned a bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Mangalore University, a master's degree in computer science from the University of Wisconsinâ€“Milwaukee, and an MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business.

## Career at Microsoft

Nadella joined Microsoft in 1992 and rose through the ranks in various engineering and business roles. Before becoming CEO, he led the company's cloud computing and enterprise business as Executive Vice President of the Cloud and Enterprise group.

### As CEO
Nadella became Microsoft's third CEO on February 4, 2014, succeeding Steve Ballmer. He also became chairman in 2021.

### Key Initiatives
Under Nadella's leadership, Microsoft has:

- Transformed into a cloud-first company with Azure
- Acquired LinkedIn for $26.2 billion
- Acquired GitHub for $7.5 billion
- Acquired Activision Blizzard for $68.7 billion
- Invested over $13 billion in OpenAI
- Integrated AI across products through Copilot

## Leadership Philosophy

Nadella is known for his emphasis on corporate culture change, introducing a "growth mindset" philosophy inspired by psychologist Carol Dweck. He shifted Microsoft's culture from internal competition to collaboration and learning.

## Book

Nadella authored "Hit Refresh" (2017), describing his vision for Microsoft's transformation and personal reflections on leadership.

## Personal Life

Nadella is married to Anupama Nadella. They have three children. His son Zain, who had cerebral palsy, passed away in 2022.
