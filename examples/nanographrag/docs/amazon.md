# Amazon.com, Inc.

Amazon.com, Inc. is an American multinational technology company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, online advertising, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. The company is headquartered in Seattle, Washington, with a second headquarters (HQ2) in Arlington, Virginia.

## History

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos on July 5, 1994, in Bellevue, Washington. The company started as an online bookstore and expanded into e-commerce, cloud services, and various technology ventures.

## Leadership

Andy Jassy became Chief Executive Officer in July 2021, succeeding founder Jeff Bezos who transitioned to Executive Chairman. Bezos later stepped down as Executive Chairman in 2023. Other key executives include:

- Brian Olsavsky, Chief Financial Officer
- Adam Selipsky, CEO of Amazon Web Services
- Doug Herrington, CEO of Worldwide Amazon Stores

## Amazon Web Services

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is the world's leading cloud infrastructure provider, ahead of Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud. AWS offers over 200 services including computing, storage, databases, and machine learning. The division generates the majority of Amazon's operating profit.

## Products and Services

Amazon operates:

- Amazon.com e-commerce marketplace
- Amazon Prime subscription service
- Prime Video streaming
- Alexa voice assistant and Echo devices
- Kindle e-readers and Fire tablets
- Ring home security (acquired 2018)
- Whole Foods Market (acquired 2017)
- MGM Studios (acquired 2022)

## Competition

Amazon competes with Walmart and Alibaba in e-commerce, with Microsoft and Google in cloud computing, with Netflix and Disney+ in streaming, and with Google Assistant and Apple Siri in voice assistants.

## AI Investments

Amazon invested $4 billion in Anthropic, the AI safety company founded by former OpenAI researchers Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei.
