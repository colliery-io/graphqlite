# Palo Alto Networks

Palo Alto Networks, Inc. is an American multinational cybersecurity company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. The company is known for its advanced firewalls and cloud-based security offerings.

## History

Palo Alto Networks was founded in 2005 by Nir Zuk, a former engineer at Check Point and NetScreen Technologies. The company pioneered next-generation firewalls that could identify and control applications regardless of port, protocol, or encryption.

## Leadership

- Nikesh Arora, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer (since 2018)
- Nir Zuk, founder and Chief Technology Officer
- Dipak Golechha, Chief Financial Officer

Nikesh Arora previously served as President and Chief Operating Officer at SoftBank and Chief Business Officer at Google.

## Products

### Network Security
- Next-generation firewalls
- SD-WAN solutions
- IoT security

### Cloud Security
- Prisma Cloud (cloud security platform)
- Prisma Access (SASE)
- Prisma SaaS

### Security Operations
- Cortex XDR (detection and response)
- Cortex XSOAR (security orchestration)
- Unit 42 (threat intelligence)

## Acquisitions

Notable acquisitions include:
- Demisto (2019) for $560 million
- CloudGenix (2020) for $420 million
- Bridgecrew (2021) for $200 million
- Cider Security (2022) for $300 million

## Competition

Palo Alto Networks competes with Fortinet, Cisco, Check Point, CrowdStrike, and Zscaler in various security segments.
