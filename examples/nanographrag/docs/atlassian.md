# Atlassian Corporation

Atlassian is an Australian-American software company headquartered in Sydney, Australia, with US headquarters in San Francisco. The company develops collaboration and productivity software.

## History

Founded in 2002 by Mike Cannon-Brookes and Scott Farquhar, college friends from the University of New South Wales. The company famously bootstrapped without venture capital initially.

## Leadership

- Mike Cannon-Brookes, Co-founder and Co-CEO
- Scott Farquhar, Co-founder and Co-CEO

## Products

### Project Management
- Jira Software - agile project management
- Jira Work Management - business teams
- Jira Service Management - IT service desk

### Collaboration
- Confluence - team wiki and documentation
- Trello - kanban-style project boards

### Developer Tools
- Bitbucket - Git repository management
- Bamboo - CI/CD server
- Sourcetree - Git GUI client

### Communication
- Loom - video messaging (acquired 2023)

## Business Model

Atlassian is known for its product-led growth model with minimal sales force, relying on self-service adoption.

## Cloud Transition

The company has been transitioning customers from Server and Data Center products to cloud-hosted solutions.

## Acquisitions

Notable acquisitions include Trello (2017, $425M), Opsgenie (2018), and Loom (2023, $975M).

## Developer Ecosystem

The Atlassian Marketplace hosts thousands of third-party apps and integrations.
