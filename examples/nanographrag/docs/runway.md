# Runway

Runway is an AI research company that develops generative AI tools for creative professionals. The company is known for pioneering AI video generation.

## History

Founded in 2018 by Cristóbal Valenzuela, Alejandro Matamala, and Anastasis Germanidis. The founders met at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP).

## Leadership

- Cristóbal Valenzuela, Co-founder and CEO
- Alejandro Matamala, Co-founder
- Anastasis Germanidis, Co-founder and CTO

## Products

### Gen-3 Alpha
Latest video generation model:
- Text-to-video generation
- Image-to-video
- High fidelity outputs
- Multiple aspect ratios

### Previous Models
- Gen-1: Video-to-video transformation
- Gen-2: Text-to-video generation

### Runway Studio
Web-based creative suite with:
- Video editing tools
- AI Magic Tools
- Green screen removal
- Image generation
- Audio tools

### API Access
Developer access to Runway models.

## Research Contributions

Runway has contributed to foundational AI research:
- Co-developed Stable Diffusion with Stability AI
- Published research on video generation
- Advances in latent diffusion models

## Use Cases

- Film and TV production
- Advertising
- Social media content
- Music videos
- Art and creative projects

## Competition

Competes with Pika, Sora (OpenAI), and other AI video generation tools.

## Industry Impact

Runway's tools have been used in major film productions, demonstrating AI's role in professional creative workflows.
