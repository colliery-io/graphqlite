# Jeff Bezos

Jeffrey Preston Bezos is an American businessman, investor, and commercial astronaut. He is the founder, executive chairman, and former president and CEO of Amazon, the world's largest e-commerce and cloud computing company.

## Early Life

Jeff Bezos was born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico. He graduated from Princeton University in 1986 with degrees in electrical engineering and computer science.

## Career

### Early Career
Before founding Amazon, Bezos worked on Wall Street at D. E. Shaw & Co., where he became the youngest senior vice president.

### Amazon
Bezos founded Amazon on July 5, 1994, in Bellevue, Washington. The company started as an online bookstore and expanded into e-commerce, cloud computing (Amazon Web Services), and numerous other businesses. Bezos served as CEO until 2021, when he transitioned to Executive Chairman.

### Blue Origin
In 2000, Bezos founded Blue Origin, an aerospace manufacturer and spaceflight services company. He flew to space aboard New Shepard on July 20, 2021.

### The Washington Post
Bezos purchased The Washington Post in 2013 for $250 million, revitalizing the newspaper with technology investments.

## Wealth

Bezos was the world's wealthiest person from 2017 to 2021. His wealth primarily derives from his approximately 10% stake in Amazon.

## Investments

Bezos has invested in numerous companies through Bezos Expeditions, including:

- Airbnb
- Twitter
- Uber
- Google (early investment)
- Arrived Homes
- Various aerospace ventures

## Philanthropy

Through the Bezos Earth Fund, he has pledged $10 billion to fight climate change. The Bezos Day One Fund focuses on homelessness and early childhood education.
